Welcome to Rajasthan

🌟 Rajasthan – The Land of Royals & Wonders 🏰✨

🌟 Rajasthan’s Iconic Cities – A Royal Journey 🏰✨

🏙 Jaipur A royal blend of history & charm! Explore Hawa Mahal, Amer Fort, and vibrant bazaars.
Udaipur – Venice of the East 🌊: A dreamy city of lakes! Enjoy City Palace, Lake Pichola, and romantic boat rides.
Jodhpur– The Blue City 🔵: A fortress of heritage! Visit Mehrangarh Fort, Umaid Bhawan, and bustling markets.
Jaisalmer– The Golden City 🏜: A desert fairytale! Discover Jaisalmer Fort, Sam Sand Dunes, and camel safaris.
Ajmer – The Spiritual Hub 🕌: A land of devotion! Seek blessings at Ajmer Sharif Dargah, Ana Sagar Lake, and Pushkar.

Beaches & Natural Beauty

🏜 Thar Desert – Golden dunes, camel safaris & mesmerizing sunsets at Sam & Khuri Sand Dunes.
⛰ Mount Abu – The only hill station! Enjoy Nakki Lake, Guru Shikhar & lush greenery.
🦜 Keoladeo National Park – A paradise for bird lovers, home to migratory birds & rare species.
🐅 Ranthambore & Sariska National Parks – Thrilling wildlife safaris to spot tigers, leopards & more.
💧 Chand Baori – A stunning ancient stepwell with intricate symmetrical architecture

Temples, Mosques & Churches

🌸 Brahma Temple (Pushkar) – The world’s only dedicated Brahma temple, beside the sacred Pushkar Lake.
🪔 Dilwara Jain Temples (Mount Abu) – Marvel at intricate marble carvings & serene spirituality.
🐀 Karni Mata Temple (Bikaner) – The famous Rat Temple, home to thousands of revered rats.
🕌 Ajmer Sharif Dargah – A revered Sufi shrine of Khwaja Moinuddin Chishti, drawing millions of devotees.
Jama Masjid (Jaipur) – A grand mosque with Mughal-style domes & minarets.

Historical Monuments

🏯 Amer Fort – A stunning blend of Rajput & Mughal architecture with grand courtyards & Sheesh Mahal.
⚔ Chittorgarh Fort – A symbol of Rajput bravery, home to Vijay Stambh & Rani Padmini’s Palace.
🏰 Kumbhalgarh Fort – The Great Wall of India, second only to the Great Wall of China!
💨 Hawa Mahal (Jaipur) – The Palace of Winds, with 953 intricate windows for royal ladies.
🏜 Jaisalmer Fort – A living fort with palaces, temples & colorful bazaars inside.
