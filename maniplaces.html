Welcome to Manipur

Manipur – Land of the Sangai & Floating Wonders 🌺🏞️

🌿 Natural Wonders & Parks

Loktak Lake & Sendra Island 🌊 The largest freshwater lake in Northeast India, known for its floating phumdis and scenic beauty.
Keibul Lamjao National Park 🦌 The world’s only floating national park, home to the endangered Sangai deer
Shirui Hills & Shirui Lily 🌸 Famous for the rare Shirui Lily, which blooms only in Manipur.

🏰 Historical & Cultural Sites

Kangla Fort 🏯 The historic fort of Manipur’s Meitei kings, located in Imphal.
INA Memorial 🏛️ A memorial dedicated to Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose and the Indian National Army.
Khongjom War Memorial ⚔️ Built in honor of Manipuri soldiers who fought against the British in 1891.

🕉️ Religious & Spiritual Places

Shree Govindajee Temple 🕉️ A significant Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Krishna and Radha.
Kaina Hill ⛰️ A sacred hill associated with Lord Krishna and Meitei religious beliefs.
Sanamahi Temple 🔥 A temple dedicated to Lainingthou Sanamahi, a deity in Meitei religion.

🏞️ Adventure & Scenic Spots

Dzukou Valley 🌿 A paradise for trekkers, famous for its stunning green landscapes and seasonal flowers.
Tharon Cave ⛏️ A fascinating limestone cave with historical significance.
Barak Waterfalls 🌊 A beautiful multi-tiered waterfall near Tamenglong district.
