🪔 Fairs and Festivals of Manipur – A Celebration of Culture and Tradition 🎉

🛕 Major Festivals of Manipur 🌟

Lai Haraoba 🙏 – Religious festival dedicated to Meitei deities.
Yaoshang (Holi) 🎨 – Five-day festival with Thabal Chongba (moonlit dance).
Ningol Chakouba 🍛 – Married women visit parental homes for a grand feast.
Kut Festival 🌾 – Harvest festival of Kuki-Chin-Mizo tribes.
Cheiraoba 🎊 – Traditional Manipuri New Year with feasting and hill climbing.
Gang-Ngai Festival 🎵 – Zeliangrong Naga festival with rituals and war dances.
Heikru Hidongba 🚣 – Religious boat race festival on Bijoy Govinda moat.

🏇2. Famous Fairs:

Manipur Sangai Festival 🎭 – Promotes tourism, handicrafts, and traditional sports.
Kang Festival (Rath Yatra) 🚩 – Chariot procession of Lord Jagannath.
Lui Ngai Ni 🌿 – Naga seed-sowing festival for agricultural blessings.
