Religions in Andhra Pradesh

Hinduism – The predominant religion in Andhra Pradesh, with a rich tradition of temples and festivals.
Islam – A significant religious community with a vibrant culture and historical influence.
Christianity – Established through colonial influence and missionary activities.
Buddhism – An ancient influence in Andhra Pradesh, with notable heritage sites like Amaravati.
Jainism – An integral part of the state's religious fabric, with historical significance.
