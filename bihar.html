Festivals like Chhath Puja, Sonepur Mela, and Sama-Chakeva reflect the deep-rooted traditions and communal harmony of the state. Linguistically diverse, the state primarily speaks Hindi, along with regional languages like Bhojpuri, Maithili, Magahi, and Angika. Today, Bihar is undergoing rapid modernization, blending its ancient traditions with advancements in education, infrastructure, and technology.
