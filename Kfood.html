Welcome to Kerala Cuisine

FOOD VARIETIES

1. Traditional Meals

Sadya – Grand vegetarian feast served on a banana leaf.
Puttu & Kadala Curry – Steamed rice cake with black chickpea curry.
Appam with Stew – Soft rice pancakes with coconut-based vegetable or meat stew.

2. Seafood Delicacies

Karimeen Pollichathu – Grilled pearl spot fish wrapped in banana leaves.
Meen Curry (Fish Curry) – Spicy and tangy fish curry made with coconut.
Chemmeen Roast – Spicy prawn roast with coconut and chilies.

3. Meat & Poultry Dishes

Nadan Kozhi Curry – Kerala-style chicken curry with aromatic spices.
Malabar Parotta with Beef Fry – Layered flatbread paired with spicy beef fry.
Erachi Varattiyathu – Slow-cooked, spicy dry meat preparation.

4. Desserts & Sweets

Payasam – Traditional Kerala sweet dish made with rice, jaggery, and coconut milk.
Unniyappam – Small, fried rice and jaggery dumplings.
Palada Pradhaman – A creamy, rice-based dessert cooked in milk.
