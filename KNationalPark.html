🏞 National Parks of Kerala 🌿🐅 – Preserving Nature’s Glory 🐅🦜

1️⃣ Periyar National Park 🐘

📍 Location: Thekkady, Idukki & Pathanamthitta
🌿 Famous For: Periyar Lake, Elephant & Tiger Reserve, Evergreen forests
🦅 Wildlife: Bengal tigers, Indian elephants, lion-tailed macaques, Malabar grey hornbills, Nilgiri langurs
🚣 Activities: Boat safari, trekking, bamboo rafting, eco-tourism, nature walks
🌟 Best Time to Visit: October to June
📌 Attractions: Periyar Lake boat ride, Border Hiking, Periyar Tiger Trail

2️⃣ Eravikulam National Park 🏔️

📍 Location: Munnar, Idukki
🌿 Famous For: Neelakurinji flowers (blooms every 12 years), Anamudi Peak
🐐 Wildlife: Nilgiri tahr, sambar deer, golden jackal, jungle cat, Indian porcupine
🚶 Activities: Trekking, wildlife photography, guided nature tours
🌟 Best Time to Visit: September to March
📌 Attractions: Anamudi Peak, Rajamalai Hills, Neelakurinji Blooming Season

3️⃣ Silent Valley National Park 🌲

📍 Location: Palakkad
🌿 Famous For: Pristine rainforest ecosystem, Kunthi River, ancient biodiversity
🦜 Wildlife: Lion-tailed macaques, Malabar giant squirrels, Nilgiri langurs, Indian pangolin
🚶 Activities: Nature walks, guided treks, birdwatching, eco-tourism
🌟 Best Time to Visit: November to May
📌 Attractions: Sairandhri Watch Tower, Kunthi River Trekking, Silent Valley Peak

4️⃣ Anamudi Shola National Park 🌿

📍 Location: Idukki
🌿 Famous For: Dense Shola forests, cool climate, rich flora
🦌 Wildlife: Indian bison (gaur), leopards, Nilgiri langurs, flying squirrels
🚶 Activities: Trekking, birdwatching, eco-trails
🌟 Best Time to Visit: October to March
📌 Attractions: Trekking to Anamudi Peak, Shola Forest Canopy Walk

5️⃣ Mathikettan Shola National Park 🌲

📍 Location: Idukki
🌿 Famous For: Rich biodiversity, dense evergreen forests, medicinal plants
🐾 Wildlife: Wild boars, leopards, Indian porcupines, civet cats
🚶 Activities: Hiking, eco-tourism, nature photography
🌟 Best Time to Visit: September to February
📌 Attractions: Unique Shola grasslands, trekking trails, panoramic viewpoints

6️⃣ Pampadum Shola National Park 🍃

📍 Location: Idukki
🌿 Famous For: Kerala’s smallest national park, biodiversity hotspot, rare orchids
🐾 Wildlife: Nilgiri martens, Indian civets, leopards, sloth bears
🚶 Activities: Nature trails, camping, birdwatching, trekking
🌟 Best Time to Visit: October to April
📌 Attractions: Birdwatching trails, scenic viewpoints, eco-tourism cottages

7️⃣ Karimpuzha National Park 🌿 (Newly Declared)

📍 Location: Malappuram
🌿 Famous For: Largest national park in Kerala, untouched forest areas, rich wildlife corridors
🐾 Wildlife: Elephants, Malabar civet, Nilgiri tahr, hornbills
🚶 Activities: Trekking, wildlife safaris, offbeat eco-tourism
🌟 Best Time to Visit: October to May
📌 Attractions: Dense rainforest trails, waterfalls, tribal settlements
